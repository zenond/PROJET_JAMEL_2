Stock-flow consistency


PStock-flow consistency links all agents' balance sheets together and guarantees that micro behaviors are correctly aggregated (Godley and Lavoie 2007).

Stocks

Household Mh + E = NW OK
Firms WIP + IN + K +Mf = Lf + Ef OK
Banks L = M + Eb OK
Deposits M = Mh +Mf OK
Loans L = Lf OK
Equities E = Ef + Eb OK
Closure NW = WIP + IN + K OK
